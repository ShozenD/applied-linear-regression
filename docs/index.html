STA 108 Applied Linear Regression


Introduction

Hello! This is my page for documenting all the projects I conducted in STA 108 Applied Linear Regresion. I took this course at the University of California Davis during the 2019 fall quarter. My instructor was Dr. Joanna C. Whitener. The purpose of this page is purely to preserve information so I can explain/show professors, employers, fellow students, and my future self what I learned through the course. Please do not copy any of the reports and use it as your own.

Course Syllabus

Textbook

Applied Linear Statistical Models - Fifth Edition (the reader), Kutner, Nachtsheim, Neter, Li. This text was recommended, but was not required.

Materials Covered

We covered selected sections of the the following chapters:

  • Chapter 1 - Linear Regression with One Predictor Variable
  • Chapter 2 - Inferences in Regression and Correlation Analysis
  • Chapter 3 - Diagnostics and Remedial Measures
  • Chapter 4 - Simultaneous Inference and Other Topics in Linear Regression
  • Chapter 6 - Multiple Regression I
  • Chapter 7 - Multiple Regression II
  • Chapter 8 - Regression Models for Quantitative and Qualitative Predictors
  • Chapter 9 - Building the Regression Model I : Model Selection and Validation
  • Chapter 10 - Building the Regression Model II : Diagnostics
  • Chapter 11 - Building the Regression Model III : Remedial Measures
